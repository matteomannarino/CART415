So, how do I play?

Lie to get ahead!

Put YOUR deception skills to the test! Frenemies gives you the opportunity to lie to your friends and dislike their online posts. You sly devil, you.

Similar to a blind date, you've got 2 choices to make: Like or Dislike!

That's right. 2 valiant teams, the Fans and the Haters, are tasked with the brave journey of posting memes on social media (my heroes!)

Oh, you like to laugh at funny pictures? Who doesn't! Frenemies provides players with a FREE web tool that generates hilarious image & captions for you. You don't even have to lift a finger! (but you do have to press a button). Use the Like and Dislike cards to vote in favor of your team!
